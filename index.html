About us

Who is behind Infinity?

A small, dedicated team focused on building an inclusive and practical learning space.

Infinity brings together students, professionals, and enthusiasts from different domains. We host meetups, hack sessions, panel talks, and casual hangouts designed to help you grow your skills and your network.

Whether you are just starting out or already experienced, you will find people to learn from, collaborate with, and get inspired by inside the Infinity community.
